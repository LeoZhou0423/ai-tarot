## Thread 2: "How I Built It" (Technical)

**Tweet 1:**
I built a free AI tarot reading app in 2 weeks.

Here's what I learned about building AI-powered products 🧵

**Tweet 2:**
Lesson 1: Prompt engineering is everything

The AI's interpretation quality depends entirely on your system prompt.

I iterated 20+ times to get:
- Personal tone (not robotic)
- Card-by-card analysis
- Coherent narrative
- Actionable guidance

**Tweet 3:**
Lesson 2: Reasoning models are different

MimoTokenPlan's reasoning model uses "thinking tokens" before answering.

This means:
- Longer response time
- Much better interpretations
- Need higher max_tokens (8192+)

**Tweet 4:**
Lesson 3: Animations matter more than you think

The card shuffle + flip animation makes the experience feel magical.

Users spend 3x longer on the app because of the animations.

Small detail, huge impact.

**Tweet 5:**
Lesson 4: Ship fast, iterate faster

MVP features:
- 5 spread types
- AI interpretations
- Save to localStorage
- Basic i18n

What I skipped:
- User accounts
- Payment
- Complex analytics

Ship first, perfect later.
