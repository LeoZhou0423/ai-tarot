# Product Hunt Launch

## Product Name
AI Tarot - Your 24/7 Mystical Advisor

## Tagline
Free AI-powered tarot readings with deep insights, available 24/7

## Description
AI Tarot combines ancient wisdom with modern AI to provide instant, personalized tarot readings. Ask any question, draw cards with beautiful animations, and receive detailed interpretations from our AI oracle.

## Key Features
- 🃏 5 spread types (Daily, Past-Present-Future, Love Pyramid, Choice, Celtic Cross)
- 🤖 AI-powered interpretations with deep insights
- ✨ Beautiful card animations and 3D flip effects
- 💬 Follow-up chat with the AI oracle
- 🌐 Bilingual support (English/Chinese)
- 📱 Mobile-friendly responsive design
- 💾 Save and history your readings
